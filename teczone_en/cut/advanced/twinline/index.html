TwinLine

Twinline is a feature in TecZone Laser that aligns edges of adjacent parts such that a single laser pass simultaneously cuts both the edges. This is achieved by keeping the gap between the adjacent parts to be equal to the kerf width. TwinLine leads to significant savings in machine time and material usage. The following example illustrates this.

Nesting without Twinline:

400 units of a part nested in TecZone Laser without Twinline option: Two sheets required with a total traverse time of 199.2 seconds

Nesting with Twinline:

The same part nested with Twinline option: Requires only one sheet. Its traverse time is 48.8 seconds.

Activation

Twinline could be used both in the and in the job workflow.

In the Job Workflow parts can be twin-lined by checking on the Twinline option in the parts tab of a job.

In the Part Workflow you need to create a single part (Shift + Click on the Quick-Nest icon). To generate several parts with twinline nesting grab one corner with the mouse and drag the corner while holding the Shift key.

Once a twinline block is created, it is currently not possible to manually explode it into independent parts.

Previewing the Sequence of Cuts

To preview the cuts and their sequence, user will have to open the Sequence tab. In this tab, all the cuts of a twinline group will be grouped together and listed in the order in which they will be cut. User can move through these items using the mouse up/down keys to play the sequence in which cuts are made.

The sequence within a twinline group cannot be modified. User will not be able to move individual/subset of the items. Also, it is not allowed to split a twinline group by dropping items into this group. Only the entire twinline group can move together.
